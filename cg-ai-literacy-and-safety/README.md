# Human-in-the-Loop: Trust, But Verify

An interactive AI ethics game where you act as an AI Systems Oversight Specialist. Face realistic scenarios and decide when to trust AI recommendations and when to override them.

## Play

[Play the game](https://sammatuba.github.io/human-in-the-loop-game)

## Features

- 10 high-stakes scenarios across 3 difficulty levels
- Automation bias simulation with investigation mechanics
- Mentor-guided narrative (Dr. Maya Chen)
- Solo and collaborative game modes
- Concept mastery quizzes
- Mobile-optimized, works offline

## AI Literacy Concepts Covered

- Automation Bias
- Training Data Bias
- AI Hallucination
- Proxy Variables
- Contextual Blindness
- Confidence vs Accuracy
- Appropriate Trust
- Human-in-Command

## Framework Alignment

UNESCO AI Competency Framework, OECD AI Principles, NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act, Council of Europe AI Literacy Framework
